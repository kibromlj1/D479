Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit Taniti?

Most visitors prefer the dry season from May to September when the weather is comfortable.

Do I need a visa to enter Taniti?

Visa requirments depends on your citizenship . For most of the coutries on arrival 30 day visa is available.

Are there ATMs on the island?

Yes there are ATM's accross the island

Is English widely spoken?

Yes in most places of the taniti island english is spoken

What type of currency is used in Taniti?

Taniti Taniti Dollar which is available in the airport for exchange
